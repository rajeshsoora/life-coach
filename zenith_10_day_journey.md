# Zenith: The 10-Day User Journey

Here is exactly what the user experiences during their first 10 days on the platform. This walkthrough illustrates how the 5 Pillars, the Agent Council, and the Growth Tree come together.

---

## 📅 Day 1: The Awakening

**Objective:** Get the user invested, assess their baseline, and show them the path forward.

1. **Sign Up & Welcome**
   - User signs up (Auth).
   - *The Orchestrator* wakes up and greets them: "Welcome to Zenith. Let's map your life."

2. **The 5-Pillar Assessment (10 minutes)**
   - The user rates themselves across MIND, BODY, WEALTH, TRIBE, and GROWTH.
   - They provide text inputs on their current pain points (e.g., "I'm stressed at work, sleeping poorly, want to lose 5kg, but I have no time.").
   - They select their preferred learning/coaching style (e.g., "Give it to me straight" vs. "I need gentle encouragement").

3. **The Reveal: The Zenith Score & Pentagon**
   - The system calculates their initial grades (e.g., MIND: C, BODY: B, WEALTH: B, TRIBE: C+, GROWTH: D).
   - The Pentagon chart is generated, showing a clear visual of their current balance.

4. **The Dynamic Roadmap & Agent Assembly**
   - Based on the assessment, the Orchestrator generates a 30-Day Roadmap focused on the user's biggest pain points.
   - The Orchestrator assigns the **Starter Team (3 Active Agents)**. Let's say:
     - 🧠 **Sleep Optimizer** (Because they mentioned poor sleep)
     - 💪 **Fitness Coach** (Because they want to lose 5kg)
     - 🌱 **Habit Coach** (Because they have no time and need routine)

5. **First Action**
   - The agents generate 3 simple tasks for Day 1 (e.g., "Go to bed at 10:30 PM", "Do a 10-min bodyweight circuit", "Log your screen time").
   - User checks off the tasks.
   - The Growth Tree goes from "Seed" to "Sprout".

---

## 📅 Day 2–3: Building Momentum

**Objective:** Establish the habit loop and demonstrate the value of the active agents.

1. **Daily Check-in**
   - User opens Zenith.
   - The Habit Coach asks: "How did yesterday go? Did you hit the 10:30 PM bedtime?"
   - User says: "No, I stayed up scrolling on my phone."

2. **Agent Collaboration**
   - The Habit Coach provides a strategy: "Let's set a 'Digital Sunset' alarm for 9:30 PM tonight."
   - The Sleep Optimizer chimes in: "Blue light suppresses melatonin. If you must use your phone, use night mode and reading glasses."

3. **Task Completion**
   - User completes Day 2 and Day 3 tasks.
   - The Growth Tree adds a few leaves.
   - Small improvements begin showing on the score (e.g., MIND score bumps from 64 to 65).

---

## 📅 Day 4–9: The Council at Work (The Loop)

**Objective:** Show the deep value of the orchestrator and the "Dormant" agents.

1. **A New Problem Arises (Day 5)**
   - User says to their Habit Coach: "I missed my workout yesterday. I had a huge fight with my partner and just felt drained."
   
2. **The Orchestrator Intervenes**
   - It reads the context ("fight with partner", "drained").
   - It scans the 62 skill cards.
   - It surfaces a nudge:
     > 💡 *Your **Conflict Resolution Coach** (TRIBE) noticed you mentioned a fight. Unresolved conflict drains energy. Want me to activate them for a 5-minute debrief?*

3. **Agent Swap (Freemium mechanic)**
   - User says yes.
   - Since they only have 3 active agent slots on the free tier, the system asks: "You can swap out an active agent, or upgrade to Zenith Pro for unlimited agents."
   - User swaps out the Habit Coach for the Conflict Resolution Coach temporarily.

4. **The Intervention**
   - The Conflict Resolution Coach walks them through a quick framework (e.g., "What was the trigger? What is their perspective?").
   - User feels better, resolves the issue.

5. **Routine Adjustments**
   - The Fitness Coach sees the missed workout and automatically adjusts the plan: "No worries about yesterday. We'll shift the heavy lifting to tomorrow. Today, just do 15 mins of yoga."

---

## 📅 Day 10: The First Milestone Review

**Objective:** Prove Zenith works, celebrate wins, and set the next stage.

1. **The 10-Day Retrospective**
   - The Orchestrator summarizes the user's progress.
   - "You've crushed 24 tasks in 10 days. Let's look at your growth."

2. **Visual Progress**
   - The Growth Tree is now a healthy "Sapling" with strong branches in BODY and MIND.
   - The Pentagon chart updates. The dent in MIND has improved slightly.

3. **Roadmap Calibration**
   - The Orchestrator asks: "Are we still focusing on sleep and fitness, or do we need to shift focus?"
   - User says: "I'm sleeping much better. Now I want to focus on my career."

4. **Agent Re-assignment**
   - The Orchestrator suggests swapping the Sleep Optimizer for the **Career Strategist**.
   - A new set of tasks is generated for the next 10 days.

---

## 🪄 The Magic of This Flow

- **It feels alive:** The agents talk to each other and react to the user's life.
- **It isn't overwhelming:** The user only deals with 3 agents at a time, but the whole system is watching their back.
- **Clear progression:** The Pentagon and the Tree provide gamified, tangible proof that their life is improving.
- **Freemium hook:** Day 5 is the perfect example of why they would want to pay — they don't want to lose their Habit Coach to talk to the Conflict Coach. They want the whole council active.
