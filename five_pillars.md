## The 5 Pillars — Detailed Breakdown

### 🧠 1. MIND — *Master Your Inner World*
> "Your mind is the operating system. Everything else runs on it."

| Sub-field | What It Covers | Agents |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Emotional Health** | Anxiety, depression, self-talk, emotional regulation | CBT Therapist, Anger Coach, Grief Companion, Confidence Builder |
| **Mental Clarity** | Meditation, breathwork, focus, mindfulness | Meditation Guide, Journaling Coach |
| **Sleep & Recovery** | Sleep quality, routines, energy management | Sleep Optimizer, Nap Strategist |
| **Habits & Discipline** | Daily routines, habit stacking, accountability | Habit Coach, Accountability Partner, Routine Architect, Digital Detox Coach |

**What "maxing MIND" looks like:**
- You sleep 7-8 hours consistently
- You have a morning routine that runs on autopilot
- You can regulate your emotions in real-time
- You meditate or journal regularly
- Your screen time is intentional, not reactive

---

### 💪 2. BODY — *Your Physical Machine*
> "You only get one body. Optimize it like your most important asset."

| Sub-field | What It Covers | Agents |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Fitness** | Strength, cardio, flexibility, sports | Fitness Coach, Yoga Guide, Running Coach, Sports Coach |
| **Nutrition** | Diet, macros, meal prep, gut health | Nutritionist, Chef, Meal Prep Strategist, Gut Health Specialist, Supplement Advisor |
| **Appearance** | Skin, hair, style, grooming | Dermatologist, Fashion Stylist, Hair Specialist, Grooming Guide, Personal Shopper |
| **Recovery** | Injury management, rest, deload | Injury Rehab Advisor, Sleep Optimizer (shared with MIND) |

**What "maxing BODY" looks like:**
- You train 3-5x/week with progressive overload
- Your nutrition matches your goals
- Your skin and grooming are dialed in
- You have zero nagging injuries
- People notice your energy

---

### 💰 3. WEALTH — *Build Your Empire*
> "Financial freedom isn't about being rich. It's about having options."

| Sub-field | What It Covers | Agents |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Career** | Job strategy, leadership, networking, public speaking | Career Strategist, Resume Optimizer, Interview Prep, Negotiation Coach, Leadership Coach, Public Speaking Coach, Networking Strategist |
| **Money** | Budgeting, investing, debt, taxes | Budgeting Coach, Investment Educator, Debt Coach, Tax Advisor, Real Estate Guide |
| **Business** | Side hustles, startups, freelancing | Startup Validator, Freelance Coach, Marketing Strategist, Sales Coach, Brand Designer |
| **Legal** | Contracts, rights, documentation | Legal Literacy Coach, Document Drafter |

**What "maxing WEALTH" looks like:**
- You earn well and your income is growing
- You save/invest consistently (30%+ of income)
- You have zero bad debt
- You have a side income stream or business idea in motion
- You can negotiate your worth confidently

---

### ❤️ 4. BONDS — *Your People, Your Tribe*
> "No one maxes out life alone. Your relationships are your multiplier."

| Sub-field | What It Covers | Agents |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Romantic** | Dating, partner communication, intimacy | Dating Coach, Relationship Counselor, Intimacy Coach |
| **Family** | Parenting, family dynamics, boundaries | Parenting Advisor, Conflict Resolution Coach |
| **Social** | Friendships, networking, community | Networking Strategist (shared), Confidence Builder (shared) |
| **Communication** | Conflict resolution, active listening, empathy | Conflict Resolution Coach, Public Speaking Coach (shared) |

**What "maxing BONDS" looks like:**
- You have a deep, communicative romantic relationship (or you're dating intentionally)
- You have 3-5 close friends you see regularly
- Family relationships are healthy (even if complex)
- You can navigate conflict without avoidance or aggression
- You feel truly connected, not lonely

---

### 🌱 5. GROWTH — *Unlock Your Next Level*
> "Growth is the only evidence of life. Stop learning, start dying."

| Sub-field | What It Covers | Agents |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Learning** | New skills, languages, reading, knowledge | Language Tutor, Coding Mentor, Speed Reading Coach, Book Companion, Debate Partner |
| **Purpose** | Ikigai, life design, meaning, direction | Ikigai Guide, Life Purpose Coach |
| **Creativity & Hobbies** | Photography, music, cooking, plants | Photography Coach, Music DJ, Chef (shared), Plant Advisor |
| **Experiences** | Travel, adventure, interior design | Travel Concierge, Interior Designer |

**What "maxing GROWTH" looks like:**
- You're actively learning something new every quarter
- You have a clear sense of purpose or direction
- You have hobbies that bring you joy (not just Netflix)
- You travel/explore regularly
- You can articulate what your ideal life looks like
